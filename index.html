About

I'm a digital marketer and builder based in Accra, Ghana. My work sits at the crossroads of growth strategy, data analytics, and software development.

During the day I lead digital campaigns at SlimFile — building analytics systems, optimizing conversion funnels, and managing growth across social, search, and email. Outside of that I build software: a community platform for sickle cell awareness, a peer-to-peer skill exchange, a grading tool, and more.

My goal is to build tools and experiences that help businesses scale while keeping the audience engaged and the data actionable.
